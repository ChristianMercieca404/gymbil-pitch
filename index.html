The idea in plain words

Every gym on the island, in one pocket.

Right now a gym membership ties you to one place. Travel across the island, switch routines, or just fancy a different setup, and you are stuck or paying twice. Gymbil makes the whole network feel like one gym. You book a slot anywhere, pay by the hour or on a network membership, unlock the door with your phone, and your workouts and progress travel with you.

For the gym goer

Freedom to train at any participating gym, with no second membership and no friction. Your history, goals and wallet are always with you.

For the gyms

A ready made booking, entry and payment system with no build cost, plus fresh revenue from quiet hours that used to sit empty.

For us

A two sided network. We earn a monthly fee from each gym plus a small commission on what flows through. The more it is used, the more it grows.

Why now, why here

Proven in Europe. Wide open in Malta.

This model already works across Europe, which takes a lot of the risk out of the concept. The twist is that the big players run a corporate benefit sold through employers, and not one of them operates in Malta. The island sits below their radar, and nobody local has claimed the space.

0
cross gym networks in Malta today. Every multi gym offer here is a single brand with a few branches, not a true network.
Speed
is the whole defence. On a small island, near total gym coverage is realistic. Lock in the density before the giants ever look this way.

Day passes already exist gym by gym at roughly ten to twenty five euro. What does not exist is a single app to book, pay and train across many independently owned gyms with one wallet and a portable record. That gap is the opening.

How it works

Three taps from sofa to squat rack.

01

Find and book

Open the app, see which gyms are near and how busy each one is right now, and reserve a slot in seconds.

02

Walk in

Your phone is the key. Unlock entry at the door, and your time is billed automatically from your wallet.

03

Train and track

Log your session, watch your progress build, and carry it all to the next gym. One record, every workout.

Where it stands

Settled, and still in motion.

Being open about what is locked and what is not. The concept, the model and the brand foundation are set. A handful of calls are still open on purpose, so nothing gets treated as final before we sign off on it.

Open questions, kept visible
